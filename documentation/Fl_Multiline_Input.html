Description

This input field displays '\n' characters as new lines rather than ^J, and accepts the Return, Tab, and up and down arrow keys. Long lines of text scroll horizontally, you may want Fl_Wordwrap_Input instead.

Because there are no scrollbars, this is probably only good for small bits of text, 10 lines at most. More powerful editing is available from the Fl_Text_Editor widget.
